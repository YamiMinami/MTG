Burgeoning

Burgeoning is a green enchantment from Magic: The Gathering that allows a player to accelerate their land drops by putting additional lands onto the battlefield whenever an opponent plays a land. This makes it a powerful card for ramp strategies, especially in multiplayer formats.


  • Detail 1: Effect: Whenever an opponent plays a land, you may put a land from your hand onto the battlefield.
  • Detail 2: Mana Cost: It costs one green mana (G), making it very efficient for its effect.
  • Detail 3:Set & Rarity: This version is from Commander 2016 (C16) and is a rare card.
